Our story

Every cake tells a story.

Ishu Creation began as a tiny home kitchen with a big dream — to make celebrations feel personal again. Today we craft each cake and gift hamper by hand, using only the freshest ingredients.

We never use artificial chemicals or unnecessary preservatives. Unlike many mass-produced market cakes, our creations are made fresh with wholesome ingredients that are safe and delicious for your family.

Every order is treated like it's for our own family. From the first sketch to the final ribbon, we obsess over the details so your moment feels effortless.

We proudly offer island-wide delivery for our beautifully curated gift boxes, bringing thoughtful surprises right to your loved ones’ doorstep. Our delicious cakes and special surprise deliveries are available throughout the Southern Province, making every celebration sweeter, warmer, and more memorable.
